Cheng Chang

I am a third year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Li Li . In 2021, I obtained my B.S. in the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University.

I am broadly interested in intelligent vehicles and autonomous driving. My current research focuses on driving scenario understanding and intelligent vehicle-infrastructure-cloud cooperative systems.
